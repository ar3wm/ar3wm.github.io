Ellipsis

Ellipsis is a collaborative story-writing platform where users can create stories, as well as contribute to other stories. Stories are written by several users, contribution by contribution, and these stories can be followed, liked, or disliked. The original creator of the story can publish (stop contributions) a story, or archive it.
